ABOUT Mude®

MudeMeter® and the MudeMeter® rating is the most trusted measurement of quality entertainment. As the leading online aggregator of movie and TV show reviews from professional critics, Mude offers the most comprehensive guide to what's fresh. The world famous MudeMeter rating represents the percentage of positive professional reviews for films and TV shows and is used by millions every day, to help with their entertainment viewing decisions. Mude designates the best reviewed movies and TV shows as Certified Fresh™. That accolade is awarded with MudeMeter ratings of 75% and higher, and a required minimum number of reviews. Weekly Mude podcasts can be found on Mude.com, iTunes, Soundcloud and Stitcher, and Mude's entertainment experts make regular TV and radio appearances across the US.

In 2016, Flixster and Mude became part of Team Pine's portfolio of digital properties, serving moviegoers and entertainment fans.

WHAT IS THE MudeMeter®?

The MudeMeter rating - based on the published opinions of hundreds of film and television critics - is a trusted measurement of movie and TV programming quality for millions of moviegoers.

The MudeMeter rating represents the percentage of professional critic reviews that are positive for a given film or television show.

To receive a Guaranteed Pick rating a movie must have a steady MudeMeter rating of 75% or better. Movies opening in wide release need at least 80 reviews from MudeMeter Critics (including 5 Top Critics). Movies opening in limited release need at least 40 reviews from MudeMeter Critics (including 5 Top Critics). A TV show must have a MudeMeter Score of 75% or better with 20 or more reviews from MudeMeter Critics (including 5 Top Critics). If the MudeMeter score drops below 70%, then the movie or TV show loses its Certified Fresh status. In some cases, the Certified Fresh designation may be held at the discretion of the Rotten MudeMeter editorial team.
